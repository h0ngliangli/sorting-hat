# Sorting Hat Video Prompts (for AI Video Generation)

## Visual Style Guide (applies to all scenes)
- **Art Style:** Warm Harry Potter film aesthetic
- **Color Palette:** Deep purple, rich gold, candlelight amber, and soft magical glows
- **Scene Setting:** Dimly lit stone hall, floating candles, ancient wooden bookshelves, and drifting magical dust particles
- **The Sorting Hat:** A weathered, expressive wizard hat matching the Harry Potter film design — frayed brim, trimmed with golden bands. The fabric folds naturally form facial features (eyes, nose, mouth). **Key: The hat sits alone on a wooden stool or pedestal with no one wearing it. No person, witch, wizard, or head beneath or inside the hat. The hat itself is the character — it speaks and moves entirely on its own.**
- **Camera:** Slow, cinematic movement — gentle push-ins, subtle orbits. No abrupt cuts
- **Mood:** Fantastical, warm, slightly dramatic, friendly and welcoming
- **Duration Target:** Each video clip approximately 5–8 seconds, seamless loop preferred
